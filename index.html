Workout Programs

Resistance

Resistance training is a form of exercise intended to increase muscular strength and endurance. It involves exercising muscles using some form of resistance. This resistance could be weights, bands, or even your own bodyweight working against gravity.

 

This can take many forms – Bodyweight, Resistance Bands, Weights, Weights Machines, Suspension Training Equipment.

 

Strength

Strength training involves the performance of physical exercises that are designed to improve strength and endurance and for developing the strength, size of muscles and maintenance of strength. It uses weighted bars, dumbbells or weight stacks for exercising the muscles.

 

Types of Weight Training- Hypertrophy Training, Powerlifting, Circuit Training, Isometric Weight Training, High Volume Training.

 

Cardio

Cardiovascular training known as Cardio, which is sometimes referred to as aerobic exercise, is any rhythmic activity that raises your heart rate into your target heart rate zone due to the repeated contraction of muscles which can improve the functioning of your heart and reduce the risk of heart disease.

 

It involves – intensive free hand exercises, jogging, jumping, running, cycling, swimming.

 
 

HIIT

HIIT (high-intensity interval training) is a type of interval training exercise which takes your cardio workout out of your comfort zone. It incorporates several rounds that alternate between several minutes of high intensity movements to significantly increase the heart rate followed by short periods of lower intensity movements.

 

It is a complete workout that combines both aerobic and strength training which maximizes your explosive performance and speed.

 

Bodyweight

Bodyweight exercises are exercises that use the person’s body weight as resistance against gravity. It does not require any free weights, machines or equipment at all. Using bodyweight was one of the original forms of strength training. Bodyweight training is easy to learn, it is effective, and can be done pretty much anywhere other than gym — at home, at work or while traveling.

 

These include – push-ups, pull-ups, sit-ups, crunches, squats, lunges, dips.

 

Calisthenics

Calisthenics is a form of strength training consisting of a variety of movements that exercise large muscle groups, such as standing, grasping, pushing, etc. These exercises are often performed rhythmically and with minimal equipment, as bodyweight exercises, intended to increase strength, fitness, endurance, coordination and flexibility.

 

It ranges from intermediate to advanced level including – plank, press-ups, inverted row, leg flutter, planche, human flag.
